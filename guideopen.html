How to Create a Fiverr Account: Step-by-Step with Screenshots
This guide walks you through creating your Fiverr account the right way—with practical screenshots and tips to help you start freelancing, even as a complete beginner!
Step-by-Step Instructions:
1
Go to Fiverr Website
Open your browser and go to fiverr.com. Click on the “Join” button at the top-right corner of the home page.
2
Enter Your Email Address
Type your email address and press “Continue”. Use your active email—you’ll need to verify it in the next step.
3
Set Your Username and Password
Pick a unique username and a strong password (include uppercase, lowercase, and numbers for security). Click “Join” or “Create Account” to continue.
Checklist / Pro-Tips:
  • Use a real and professional-looking profile photo.
  • Be specific about your skills and experiences in your bio.
  • Choose a username you’re proud of—it’s permanent!
  • Keep all account information accurate and up-to-date.
  • Don’t use fake emails or sketchy info—your account will get banned.
